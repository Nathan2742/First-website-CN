EARLY GAME - Vel'Koz's level 6 is a massive powerspike as it lets him utilize his passive fully. It is a great finishing ability that is best used against enemies who are clumped together. His first item will give a massive boost to his overall effectiveness in the lane. It will increase his burst damage combo, and he can force enemies to recall abruptly quite frequently in the lane.

MID GAME - He will have two points in his Ultimate R once he hits level 11. This means that Vel'Koz will use the Ultimate R more efficiently as a finishing move to get rid of low health targets.Team fights are going to be really good for Vel'Koz due to his kit. If he manages to score a knock upon a target, he will probably kill them instantly with his team.

LATE GAME - At level 16, Vel'Koz will have his Ultimate R maxed out. This means that he can now deal tons of damage from far away even if he gets one stack of his Passive on his enemy. His damage output will be really high due to the number of items he possesses at that point in the game. He should focus on using the fog of war to poke out enemies first. Vel'Koz will be extremely good in team fights, especially fights where he is playing around choke points. This means that he can apply his passive stacks on multiple enemies, making it easier for him to kill a bunch of enemies together.

Strengths & Weaknesses

The strengths to Vel'Koz:

  • High poke damage
  • Extremely good wave clear
    • Allows more time for objectives and help the team
  • Excels in fights
    • Close quater fights means he can stack his passive faster
  • Force enemy player to stay behind minions in lane phase

The weaknesses to Vel'Koz:

  • Very immobile making him vuulnerable
  • If pushed to much in lane potential to die from junlger

  • His Ultimate R roots him in palce
